Thunkable is a platform that allows users to create mobile apps without requiring extensive programming knowledge. It provides an intuitive drag-and-drop interface that allows users to create apps by assembling pre-built components. The platform supports both Android and iOS app development, and users can also publish their apps directly from the platform. Thunkable also provides various templates and tutorials to help users get started with app development, and it has a community forum where users can share their projects and get help from other users. Thunkable is used by individuals, businesses, and educational institutions to create a wide range of apps, from simple utilities to complex enterprise applications.
