Instagram is a social media platform that allows users to share photos and videos with their followers. It was launched in 2010 and quickly gained popularity, with over 1 billion monthly active users as of 2021. Instagram allows users to edit and enhance their photos and videos using filters and other tools, and also provides a variety of features such as stories, reels, IGTV, and live streaming. Users can follow other accounts to see their posts in their feed, and can also interact with posts by liking, commenting, and sharing them. Instagram has become a popular platform for businesses and influencers to reach and engage with their audiences.
